Mysterious Man; I have given thee a power to manipulate features of Mana and Ki. You have the power to increase your power by literally doing nothing. In your menu, left click a stat to increase it. Good Luck. Some stats cannot be increased by clicking. Some stats will require activities that will drain your Stamina. Once your stamina is gone you will no longer have the ability to do move or fight and you must wait for your stamina to regenerate.
